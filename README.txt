Aymandeghedy Multi-Page Portfolio (Google Drive links - 23 videos)

This package is small and downloads reliably.
Your videos stay on Google Drive.

✅ What you MUST do:
1) In Google Drive folder: Share → Anyone with the link → Viewer
2) On hosting: edit videos.json
   - Paste each Drive link into drive_view_link
   - Save
3) Open: portfolio.html and click Play

Drive link formats supported:
- https://drive.google.com/file/d/FILE_ID/view?usp=sharing
- https://drive.google.com/open?id=FILE_ID

The website auto converts them to /preview.
